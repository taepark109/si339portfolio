ABOUT ME

Taejung Park

Nationality: South Korean

Major: BSI


Who am I?


My name is Taejung Park. I am a student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I hope to study the science of information, and am a prospect for the UMSI program. Also, I am preparing for a minor in Business, so that I can combine both business and information science as fuel to excel my experience in the workplace.


How I Became Interested in UX and UI


My interest in UMSI was a decision I made in the winter term of my freshman year, when I discovered that I wanted to incorporate aspects of design in addition to writing code, thus leading me towards UI and UX design.


Where I Want To Be Years From Now


Having moved from South Korea at a young age, I wish to go back and delve in the UX field there as well as explore the cultural differences. I hope to bring what I learn here at the University of Michigan abroad and make an impact on making international design accessible for all people.


More About Me


Outside of my education, I enjoy several hobbies. These include dancing, lifting weights, drawing, fashion, interior design, and many other activities and interests.
